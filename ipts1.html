DOCUMENTS PREPARATION

Please prepare the following materials.

1. Passport:

Your current passport must not expire within the next six months and must have at least two blank visa pages.

2. Application Form:

  • No hand written applications will be accepted (TYPED ONLY).
  • No blank answers, type N/A if the question is not applicable for you.
  • Give complete information requested (names, address, andphone numbers).

*(If you do not know how to fill out the VISA application form, you can contact us online or you can come to our office, IPTS will help you to finish the application form together).

>>Click here to download the visa application

3. Passport Photo:

  • 2 X 2 inches (51 X 51 mm) in size
  • Photos must be taken within six months
  • Photos must have white back ground with full face from view.

*(If you do not know where to take the passport photos, you can come to our office, IPTS offer passport photo service)

4. Proof of Travel:

Submit an itinerary from a travel agency or online booking agency showing departure from the U.S. and arrive/departure China.

5. Authorization Form:

Please sign up the authorization letter for VISA to grant permission to IPTS.

>>Click here to download the authorization form

6. Letter of invitation*(ONLY FOR BUSNIESS VISA)

The letter should provide specific information, must be signed by an appropriate representative of the inviting organization and must be stamped with the company seal.

>>Click here to download the authorization form
